Georgia Open Records — Key Facts

  • Agencies must acknowledge requests within 3 business days.
  • Records must be produced within a reasonable time (typically cited as ~3 business days for simple requests).
  • Requesters do not need to state a reason for their request.
  • Some records are exempt (e.g., ongoing investigations, personal medical info, trade secrets).
  • Submit online: foia.atlantaga.gov
  • Procurement records: openrecords-procurement@atlantaga.gov
  • General info: atlantaga.gov open records page
  • Georgia AG guide: law.georgia.gov
Response deadline reminder: After submitting, note the date. If you haven't heard back in 3 business days, follow up with the department's records custodian.
